How to get your Snapchat export

It takes two minutes to ask, and Snapchat usually emails you within a few hours (sometimes up to a day).

  1. 1 In Snapchat, tap your profile → the gear ⚙️ (Settings) → scroll to My Data → Submit Request. (You can also do it at accounts.snapchat.com.)
  2. 2 Make sure “Include Memories and other files” is ticked, then submit. Snapchat emails you a link when it's ready.
  3. 3 Download the file(s) to your computer. A big library often arrives as several ZIP files (data-1.zip, data-2.zip…). Put them all in the same folder. You don't need to unzip them.
  4. 4 Open SnapRescue, click Choose folder, and pick that folder (the one holding the ZIPs). That's it.

💡 Already unzipped them? That works too. Point SnapRescue at the folder that contains your media and the memories_history.json file.
